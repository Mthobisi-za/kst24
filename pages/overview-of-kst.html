Founding Partners

Cyril Ramaphosa Foundation

Cyril Ramaphosa Foundation (the Foundation), through its partner entities in small business development, youth development and education, aims to foster an empowered and inclusive society. Adopt-a- School Foundation, a partner entity of the Foundation, was the co-implementing partner for the development and implementation of the DWSD programme.

Kagiso Trust

Kagiso Trust (KT) is a leading development agency working towards a prosperous, peaceful, equitable and just society. The Beyers Naude Schools Development Programme, a programme of KT, was the co-implementing partner for the development and implementation of the District Whole School Development programme.

10 YEARS OF TRANSFORMATIVE IMPACT

Over the past ten years, KST has steadfastly driven institutional and systemic change through its innovative District Whole School Development (DWSD) model. This approach, which integrates support for infrastructure, curriculum, leadership, and social welfare, has proven to be a powerful catalyst for educational improvement.

Theory of Change

If the educator receives the relevant training, support and learning materials and the school environment is improved through better infrastructure and improved safety methods, and leadership within the school and district offices are strengthened, the quality of learner results will inevitably improve.

Sustainability roadmap

From the outset, KST has prioritised embedding sustainability mechanisms and processes within its District Whole School Development model, recognising the programme’s defined lifespan. Sustainability strategies are integrated into every element of the model to ensure long-lasting benefits and impact even after the programme concludes. A critical aspect of this approach is empowering systems leaders and establishing systemic frameworks and practices across schools and district offices. This ensures leadership capacity is strengthened and sustained within the education system.
